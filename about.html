About PIA

Pakistan International Airlines (PIA) is the national flag carrier of Pakistan and one of the oldest airlines in Asia. Founded in 1955, PIA has played a vital role in connecting Pakistan with the world, pioneering aviation in the region.

PIA's headquarters are located in Karachi, and the airline operates a vast network of domestic and international flights to Asia, Europe, North America, and the Middle East. With over six decades of aviation excellence, PIA continues to uphold a strong legacy built on trust, reliability, and innovation.
