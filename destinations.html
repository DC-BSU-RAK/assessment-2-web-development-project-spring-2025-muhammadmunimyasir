Explore Our Destinations

PIA proudly connects Pakistan to the world with a vast network of domestic and international destinations. Whether you’re flying for business, leisure, or religious purposes, our flights cover the routes that matter most.

London, United Kingdom

Direct flights from Islamabad, Lahore, and Karachi to Heathrow. Perfect for both business and leisure travelers.

Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

A key destination for Hajj and Umrah travelers with seasonal promotions and dedicated services.

Dubai, UAE

Frequent flights for business and tourism with seamless travel options from Pakistan’s major cities.

Toronto, Canada

PIA connects the Pakistani diaspora in Canada with non-stop flights from Lahore and Islamabad.

Skardu, Pakistan

A gateway to the northern mountains, Skardu is a breathtaking destination for tourists and adventurers.

Karachi, Pakistan

The airline’s central hub offering both international and domestic connections across the globe.
