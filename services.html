Our Services

At Pakistan International Airlines (PIA), we aim to offer passengers a safe, comfortable, and reliable travel experience. We provide a wide range of services designed to meet the diverse needs of our passengers, both on the ground and in the air.

  • ✈️ Domestic & International Passenger Flights

    We offer regular flights across Pakistan and to major cities around the world. Passengers can choose between Economy, Economy Plus, and Business Class cabins, all designed with comfort in mind.

  • 🍽️ In-Flight Meals & Entertainment

    Enjoy complimentary hot meals on international routes, with a selection of dietary options. In-flight entertainment includes movies, music, and documentaries (available on select aircraft).

  • 📱 Online Booking & Mobile App

    Use our website or mobile app to book tickets, check flight status, manage bookings, and check-in online. Mobile boarding passes and real-time notifications are also available.

  • 💼 Corporate Travel Solutions

    We offer tailored solutions for businesses and organizations, including flexible booking, account managers, and discounted travel packages.

  • 🧳 Baggage Services

    Generous baggage allowances across all classes. Special handling for oversized items, musical instruments, and sports equipment is also available.

  • 🛬 Hajj & Umrah Travel

    We provide seasonal flights for religious pilgrimage with priority boarding, additional baggage for Zamzam water, and dedicated airport staff to assist pilgrims.

  • 🚚 Cargo Services

    Efficient domestic and international cargo services, including express delivery, perishables, and medical shipments with real-time tracking.

  • 🛫 Charter Flights

    PIA offers private charter flight services for corporate clients, sports teams, and religious groups with customizable itineraries.

  • 🧍 Special Assistance

    We provide assistance for passengers with reduced mobility, unaccompanied minors, and elderly travelers, ensuring a stress-free experience.

  • 🌐 Airport Lounges

    Business Class passengers and frequent flyers can enjoy access to PIA lounges featuring refreshments, free Wi-Fi, and quiet workspaces.
